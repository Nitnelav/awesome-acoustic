About

Hello! I'm an enthusiast of open-source and acoustic research. I created Awesome Acoustic to help the community discover, share, and contribute to the best open-source acoustic software and resources. My goal is to make it easier for researchers, engineers, and hobbyists to find and collaborate on high-quality acoustic tools.

If you have suggestions or want to connect, feel free to open an issue or pull request on GitHub!
